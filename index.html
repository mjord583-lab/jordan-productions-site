LED Walls Built for Impact

From corporate keynotes to branded environments, our LED video walls deliver vivid, high-resolution visuals at any scale. We handle everything — panel configuration, content formatting, and on-site calibration.

  • Indoor and outdoor panels from 2.6mm to 10mm pixel pitch
  • Custom layouts including curved and angled configurations
  • Real-time content switching and multi-source inputs
  • On-site technician for the duration of your event

Sound That Reaches Every Seat

Precision-tuned PA systems engineered for speech intelligibility and musical clarity. Our audio engineers use Behringer Wing digital consoles for precise mixing and seamless multi-channel control.

  • Line array and point-source systems for any venue size
  • Behringer Wing digital mixing with wireless monitoring
  • Wireless microphone systems (Shure, Sennheiser)
  • Sub-bass reinforcement and delay fills for large spaces

Lighting That Sets the Tone

Our lighting designers craft atmospheres that transform venues and elevate every moment. From elegant uplighting to dramatic beam effects, each rig is programmed to your event's unique flow.

  • Intelligent moving heads and beam fixtures
  • LED wash and architectural uplighting
  • Cue-by-cue programming synced to your run-of-show
  • Truss, rigging, and haze for complete production looks

Who We Are

Built on Experience, Driven by Precision

Jordan Productions is a full-service media and live event production company based in the Philadelphia area. For nearly a decade, we've delivered audio, video, and lighting solutions for organizations that demand reliable, high-quality AV production.

We work directly with event planners, producers, and corporate teams to translate their vision into a technical plan — then execute every detail from load-in through load-out. No guesswork, no surprises.

Whether it's a 5,000-seat keynote, a community festival, a university commencement, or a permanent house of worship installation, we bring the same standard of preparation and professionalism to every project.

How We Work

From Concept to Curtain Call

A proven four-phase approach that keeps your event on track and every stakeholder aligned.

01

Discovery & Planning

We start with your goals — audience, venue, timeline, budget. Site walkthroughs, technical specs, and a detailed production plan follow.

02

Design & Preparation

Equipment selection, content formatting, lighting plots, and run-of-show scripting. Every detail mapped before we arrive on site.

03

On-Site Execution

Our crew handles load-in, setup, testing, and live operation. A dedicated project manager coordinates every cue and vendor throughout.

04

Post-Event Review

Debrief, performance notes, and recommendations for future events. We build long-term partnerships, not one-off transactions.
